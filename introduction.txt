#Tank control
W       -       Forward
S       -       Backward
A       -       Turn left
D       -       Turn right
ENTER   -       Shoot
ESC     -       Quit

#Camera control
U       -       Orbital camera up
O       -       Orbital camera down
I       -       Orbital camera forward
K       -       Orbital camera backward
J       -       Orbital camera left
L       -       Orbital camera right

UP      -       Overlooking camera forward
DOWN    -       Overlooking camera backward
LEFT    -       Overlooking camera left
RIGHT   -       Overlooking camera right

#Scene control
1       -       Change current camera to Orbital camera
2       -       Change current camera to Overlooking camera
P       -       Change the lighting mode
F       -       Change the fog mode
